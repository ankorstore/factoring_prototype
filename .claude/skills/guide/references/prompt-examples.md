# Prompt Examples for Designers

Curated prompts organized by task. Copy-paste or adapt these when talking to Claude.

## Creating Pages

- "Create a dashboard page with 4 stat cards showing revenue, orders, customers, and conversion rate"
- "Create a product catalog page with a grid of cards, each showing image placeholder, name, price, and stock badge"
- "Create a settings page with sections for profile info, notifications preferences, and account actions"
- "Create a checkout flow with 3 steps: cart review, shipping address form, and payment confirmation"
- "Create a page like the order list but for managing team members with role badges"

## Working with Tables

- "Add a data table with columns for name, email, role, status, and last login"
- "Create a sortable product table with filters for category and price range"
- "Add pagination to the table — show 10 items per page"
- "Add a search input that filters the table rows as I type"

## Forms & Inputs

- "Create a form with name, email, phone, and a role dropdown"
- "Add a modal that asks 'Are you sure?' before deleting an item"
- "Create an address form with street, city, zip code, and country select"
- "Add toggle switches for email notifications, SMS alerts, and marketing opt-in"

## Layout & Styling

- "Switch this page to the connected layout (backoffice sidebar)"
- "Make this layout responsive — 1 column on mobile, 3 columns on desktop"
- "Add a card with a white background, rounded corners, and subtle border"
- "Use the success color for the 'Active' badge and error color for 'Cancelled'"

## Design Tokens

- "What colors are available in the design system?"
- "Show me the spacing scale"
- "Change the card background to warm-white"
- "Use the DS border radius tokens instead of hardcoded pixels"

## Data & Fixtures

- "Create mock data for a list of 20 products with name, price, category, and stock"
- "Add a JSON fixture with 15 sample invoices"
- "Create TypeScript interfaces for the product data"

## Deployment

- "Deploy this prototype to GitHub Pages"
- "Build the project and check for errors"
